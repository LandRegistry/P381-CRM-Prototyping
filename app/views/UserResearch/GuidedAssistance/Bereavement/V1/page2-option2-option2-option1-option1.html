How did we arrive at this recommendation?

  • The land or property of the person who died is not registered with us.
  • The person who died was not a joint owner of the land or property.
  • You do not want to transfer/assent or sell the land or property of the person who died.
  • The transfer is to a beneficiary of the person who died.
  • You want to transfer the land or property.
