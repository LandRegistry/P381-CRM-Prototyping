How to update property records when someone has died

Is the land or property of the person who died registered with HM Land Registry?

Do you want to transfer/assent or sell the land or property of the person who died?

An Assent of land or property is the transfer of the legal ownership from the Estate of someone who has died, into the name of the new owner.

Was the person who died a joint owner of the land or property?

Is the transfer to a beneficiary of the person who died?

A beneficiary is a person who is set to inherit from the property of someone who has died.
