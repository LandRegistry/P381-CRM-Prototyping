How to update property records when someone has died

We recommend that you visit

transfer unregistered land or property to the surviving owner and a third party

We recommend this action because:

  • the land or property of the person who died is not registered with us
  • you do not want to transfer/assent or sell the land or property of the person who died
  • the transfer is not to a beneficiary of the person who died
  • the person who died was a joint owner of the land or property
  • the surviving owner wants to transfer the land or property
