How to update property records when someone has died

Please consult the following information:

Transfer property to a third party after the death of a sole owner

We recommend this action because:

  • The person who died was not a joint owner of the land or property.
  • The transfer is not to a beneficiary of the person who died.
  • The land or property of the person who died is registered with us.
  • You want to transfer/assent or sell the land or property of the person who died.
