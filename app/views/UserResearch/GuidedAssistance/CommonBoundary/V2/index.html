Deal with common boundary matters

There may be some boundary enquiries where HM Land Registry is unable to help because the matter falls outside of our responsibilities. In these instances we'll direct you to an organisation who we believe may be able to help.

Use this service to find out

  • if we can help with your boundary enquiry
  • how to access and interpret any information we hold
  • what your options are when we are unable to help
  • how to correct a mistake on your title plan

Before you start, you will need to know

  • if the property is registered with HM Land Registry
  • who owns the property
  • whether it's owned jointly or by one person

If you do not know this information, you can search for property information here.

! Warning We only deal with land and property in England and Wales.
There are different rules for Scotland and Northern Ireland
