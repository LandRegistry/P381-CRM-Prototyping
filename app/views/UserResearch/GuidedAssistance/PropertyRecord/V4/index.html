How to change your name on property records

The majority of land and property in England and Wales is registered with HM Land Registry. The register is a legal record of who owns the land or property and is available to the public to view.

Use this service to find out

  • what forms you'll need to complete
  • what documents you'll need to send

Before you start, you will need to know

  • if the property is registered with HM Land Registry
  • whether the property is owned jointly or by one person

You can search for information about a property from HM Land Registry.

! Warning We only register land and property in England and Wales.
We do not manage the registers for Scotland and Northern Ireland
