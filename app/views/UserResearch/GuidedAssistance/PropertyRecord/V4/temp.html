How to change your name on property records

Is the property registered with HM Land Registry?

Why do you want you to update a property record?

Divorce or end of civil partnership

You need to change your name on the property register after divorce or the end of a civil partnership

Marriage or civil partnership

You need to change your name on the property register after marriage or civil partnership

Change of name by deed poll

You need to update the property register after a change of name by deed poll

Correct a spelling error

You need to update your name on the property register to correct a spelling error

Gender change

You need to update your name on the property register after changing gender

To match passport or driving licence

You need to change your name on the property register to match your passport or driver’s licence

Ownership has changed

You need to change the name on the property register to the new owner’s name
