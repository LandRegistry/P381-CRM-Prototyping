Business Regulatory details

Is your organisation a regulated or licensed body?

An organisation (unless they are a government department) applying for Network Services must be or must employ a person authorised to carry out conveyancing and land registration work under the Legal Services Act 2007, Part 3 and Schedule 2 paragraphs 5 (1) (a) and (b) (the 'relevant reserved instrument activities').

This information is required if applying for an NAA. All references to the LR (NA) Rules mean the Land Registration (Network Access) Rules 2008 as amended by Land Registration (Network Access) (Amendment) Rules 2011.

Who is your organisation's regulator or licensing authority?

If your organisation is a recognised body or a licensed body, please provide details of your regulator or licensing authority.

Does your organisation empoloy an authorised person?

If your organisation is not a recognised body or a licensed body or a government department, you must certify that your organisation employs at least one authorised person, as defined in section 18 of the Legal Services Act 2007, who is entitled to carry on relevant reservced instrument activities and who will carry on or direct and supervise those activities.
