Sign your application

Before you sign, check your eligibilty:

  • I am authorised to make this application on behalf of the organisation.
  • The organisation meets the minimum technical specification for equipment and/or software.
  • Minimum technical specification for equipment. The customer must have the appropriate IT equipment and security arrangements. These are set out in the technical manual.
  • I accept the terms of the Conditions of Use on the organisation's behalf, as exist at the date of this application. It is acknowledged that these may change from time to time.
  • The Conditions of Use apply to your access to services other than Network Services, including HM Land Registry's electronic Document Registration Service and Information Services. The Network Access Agreement applies to your access (if any) to Network Services.
  • This application must be signed and dated on behalf of the organisation by a duly authorised person within the organisation such as a company director, company secretary, partner or equivalent.

To sign your application you need to:

  1. Sign and print your name on a blank white piece of paper using black ink.
  2. Date your signature using black ink.
  3. Take a photo or scan a copy of the signature, printed name and date.
  4. Attach the photo or scanned copy below.
