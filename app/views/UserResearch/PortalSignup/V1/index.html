Apply for or update a
Business e-services account

Important

This service will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity and any work will be lost. There is no option to save and resume an application

Use this service:

  • apply for access to Information Services - Including Application Enquiry and MapSearch, Land Charges Services and the electronic Document Registration Service (e-DRS)
  • apply for access to Network Services - available to legal services providers, such as solicitors and licensed conveyancers
  • apply for access to Lender Services - available for lenders and third party agents that sometimes act on their behalf
  • update account details for organisations that already have a Business e-services account

Before you start, you will need:

  • to have read and understood your obligations in the Conditions of Use (COU)
  • the name and contact details for your organisation, administrators, responsible person and point of contact
  • an existing variable Direct Debit (VDD) key number for your organisation or if you donâ€™t have one, fill in a VDD application form and direct debit mandate (see pages 13 to 15 of the VDD guide) for each account you require
  • a completed and signed paper copy of the Network Access Agreement (NAA), if applying for Network Services
  • a completed and signed paper copy of the Memorandum of Understanding (MOU), if applying for Lender Services
  • the ability to scan or take a photo and upload a signature
  • the ability to scan and upload an NAA, VDD and MOU form, if applicable
