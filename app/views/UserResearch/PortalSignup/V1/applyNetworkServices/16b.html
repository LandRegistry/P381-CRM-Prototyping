Does your organisation employ an authorised person?

If your organisation is not a recognised body, a licensed body or a government department, you must certify that your organisation employs at least one authorised person, as defined in section 18 of the Legal Services Act 2007, who is entitled to carry on relevant reserved instrument activities and who will carry on or direct and supervise those activities.
