Sign your application

This application must be signed and dated on behalf of the organisation by the existing responsible person or a duly authorised person within the organisation such as a company director, company secretary, partner or equivalent.

We can only accept your application if you have followed these steps:

  1. Sign and print your name on a blank white piece of paper using black ink.
  2. Date your signature using black ink.
  3. Take a photo or scan a copy of the signature, printed name and date.
  4. Attach the photo or scanned copy below.
! Warning Your application will be rejected if you do not follow these steps.
