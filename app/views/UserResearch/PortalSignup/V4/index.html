Apply for or update a Business
e-services account

Our Business e-services are for organisations that regularly transact with us as part of the conveyancing process to request information from HM Land Registry’s Land Register or to lodge applications to change the register online.

Select from one of the following options:

Applying for a standard account will allow access to services such as:

  • Application Enquiry
  • MapSearch
  • Land Charges Services
  • electronic Document Registration Service (e-DRS)
  • to have read and understood your obligations in the Conditions of Use (COU)
  • the name and contact details for your organisation, administrators,
    responsible person and point of contact
  • an existing variable Direct Debit (VDD) key number for your organisation or
    if you don’t have one, fill in a VDD application form and direct debit mandate
    (see pages 13 to 15 of the VDD guide) for each account you require
  • the ability to scan or take a photo and upload a signature
  • the ability to scan and upload a VDD form

Applying for an advanced account, if applicable, will allow you to access:

  • Network Services - available to legal services providers, such as solicitors and licensed conveyancers
  • Lender Services - available for lenders and third-party agents that sometimes act on their behalf
  • to have read and understood your obligations in the Conditions of Use (COU)
  • the name and contact details for your organisation, administrators,
    responsible person and point of contact
  • an existing variable Direct Debit (VDD) key number for your organisation or
    if you don’t have one, fill in a VDD application form and direct debit mandate
    (see pages 13 to 15 of the VDD guide) for each account you require
  • a completed and signed paper copy of the Network Access Agreement
    (NAA)    , if applying for Network Services
  • a completed and signed paper copy of the Memorandum of Understanding
    (MOU)    , if applying for Lender Services
  • the ability to scan or take a photo and upload a signature
  • the ability to scan and upload an NAA, VDD and MOU form, if applicable

This will allow you to:

  • Add an administrator
  • Add a deputy responsible person
  • Change your responsible person
  • Create a new variable Direct Debit (VDD) account key number
  • Change the bank account details of a current variable Direct Debit account
  • Apply for Network Services
  • Apply for Lender Services
