  1. Access to Network Services (NAA)

    What is Network Services?
    An organisation (unless they are a government department), applying for Network Services must be or must employ a person authorised to carry out conveyancing and land registration work under the Legal Services Act 2007, Part 3 and Schedule 2 paragraphs 5 (1) (a) and (b) (the ‘relevant reserved instrument activities’).

    If applying for Network Services, you are required to provide details of your organisations regulator or licensing body OR complete the authorised person certificate. See paragraph 1, (1) (a) and (b) of Schedule 1 to the Land Registration (Network Access) Rules 2008 (as amended by the Land Registration (Network Access) (Amendment) Rules 2011).
