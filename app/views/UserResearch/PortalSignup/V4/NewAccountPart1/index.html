Business e-services account application

To apply for a Business e-services account, you need to provide details for a primary administrator, responsible person and point-of-contact. A single-point-of-contact can be used for all 3, if required.

The responsibilities for these roles are listed below.

Role Responsibilities
Primary administrator Your administrator will create and manage accounts for your organisation's authorised users.
Responsible person The responsible person will:
  • supervise the administrators
  • get access to reports documenting the activities of the administrators
  • access VDD account data
Point of contact We will contact this person if there's a query with your application.
