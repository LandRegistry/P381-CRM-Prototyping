Contact HM Land Registry: Large Scale Application

Large Scale Applications usually affect commercial clients such as banks, UK and oversea companies and other corporate bodies. Typically, they are based on a common deed, such as a bulk transfer or charge, or a change of name or address comprising:

  • more than 50 registered titles; or
  • more than 50 unregistered properties; or
  • any combination of registered and unregistered titles exceeding 50 in total.

If your enquiry does not fall within the above then use Contact HM Land Registry: General Enquiry
